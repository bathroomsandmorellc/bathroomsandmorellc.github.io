Frequently asked questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

What is Bathrooms and More, LLC?
Bathrooms and More, LLC is a family-owned business based in Great Falls, MT that specializes in designing and constructing high-end, custom walk-in showers.
What makes Bathrooms and More unique?
Bathrooms and More offers an installer-direct service model, eliminating middlemen and providing a more efficient and cost-effective experience for clients.
What materials do you use for installations?
We hand-pick a range of high-end materials, including natural stone, Fibo wall panels, and fixtures from renowned brands, to ensure luxury and durability in your bathroom.
What services does Bathrooms and More offer?
Bathrooms and More offers design services, installation services, material selection, and consultation and advice to transform your bathroom into a luxurious sanctuary.
How can I contact Bathrooms and More?
You can get in touch by using the form on our website, calling (406) 640-3089, or emailing info@bathroomsandmorellc.com. You can also follow us on Facebook and Instagram for updates on our latest projects.
